Changing the world with trees

The Miniforest Foundation is dedicated to pushing for a better Earth and saving the environment through the help of people just like you.
Think about this: trees and plants help us a *lot*. They bring oxygen into the atmosphere, help with the water cycle, create new habitats for wild animals, and support ecosystems by a huge margin. So if the Earth helps us so much, why not help the Earth? Doing something as small as planting a flower in that unused path of grass on your front lawn all the way to planting millions of trees on the side of highways all help to promote our cause.

The Miniforest Foundation is based on the Miyawaki method.

Miyawaki forests were created by a Japanese botanist named Akira Miyawaki. They require minimal space but are great ways to introduce new species and create strong ecosystems. You can read more about them here and here.
